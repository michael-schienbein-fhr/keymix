What is this Keymix?

Keymix lets you generate playlists using Spotify's recommedation engine using a variety of interesting playlist seed options. You can then save them to your Keymix account and from there you have the option of transferring them to your Spotify account.


1. Start by registering an account.
2. By clicking the button below, you can start creating mixes or using the search tab above.
3. You can also choose to link your Spotify account first, for saving generated results straight to that account.
